About Us

Learn more about our business

Crafting Creativity with Purpose

IFC Crafts is a handmade craft business run by student artists, dedicated to providing customers with unique, high-quality, and personalized products such as necklaces, keychains, and henna art. The business aims to deliver a more organized and efficient experience by making product browsing, pricing, and ordering easier and more accessible. Through creativity and craftsmanship, IFC Crafts connects local artistry with customers in a more structured and convenient way.
